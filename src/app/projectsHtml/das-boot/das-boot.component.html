3D Printed Trophy from the Cannella-Stowers Annual Das Boot Championchip

Every super bowl my brother-in-law and I have a competition to see who can drink the boot the fastest. This year I decided to create a commemorative Das Boot Trophy in SolidWorks

This trophy was heavilly based on a this soldiworks tutorial. It took about 27 hrs to print with conservative setting on the Ender3 3D printer and just barely fit onto the build platform.
